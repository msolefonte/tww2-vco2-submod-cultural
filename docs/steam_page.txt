[h2]Introduction[/h2]
This mod brings back the Cultural Victory to the Mortal Empires campaign. It can work as a standalone mod or together with their brothers the [url=#]Main Mod[/url], the [url=#]Economic Victory Submod[/url] and the [url=#]Military Victory Submod[/url].

[h2]New Victory Conditions[/h2]
The objectives are based on the following pattern: Own 30 provinces, research an 80% of your technology tree and maintain at least 51% of your religion in your owned provinces. In the case of different technology tree inside of the same race (Repanse of Wulfhart), the smaller tree has been used as base.

The complete list can be found [url=https://github.com/msolefonte/tww2-vco2-submod-cultural/blob/tree/docs/README.md]here[/url].
